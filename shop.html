Golden Apple Residence.

Golden Apple Residence is a 4th-generation high-rise residential development comprising two elegant blocks of 23 floors each. Thoughtfully designed for modern urban living, the project seamlessly blends functionality, comfort, and refined aesthetics, making it ideal for both homeowners and investors. Located in a prime area, Golden Apple Residency offers residents stunning views, top-notch amenities, and easy access to essential services.
